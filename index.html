About This Website

The Scholarship Department of Swami Ramanand Teerth Marathwada University, Nanded, is committed to supporting students in securing financial assistance for their education.

Important note

  • Fail TC, sem stage admission is also not eligible for Post matric and tuition and exam fees
  • Eligibility form scholarship: If the degree is professional and the admission is taken in a non-professional course, such a student will not be eligible for both the non-professional course scholarship and freeship.
